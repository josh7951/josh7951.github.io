About

A little bit about myself . . . I was born and raised in the high desert of southern California. I graduated from the top of my class at William J. "Pete" Knight High School in 2012. After graduating, I briefly attended California Polytechnic State University, Pomona for Aerospace Engineering. Around the spring time of 2014, I was hospitalized with a random heart condition called Acute Pericarditis. The stress from the issues caused me to fall behind in school and I made the difficult decision of taking a leave of absence from Cal Poly Pomona to catch up on my GE's at a local Community College, named College of the Canyons, to be closer to home and to also save money to cover medical costs. During my leave of absence, I mistakenly missed a deadline to extend my leave of absence or re-enroll in courses, and I was effectively dropped from the program. I did not let this stop me. I continued my education at College of the Canyons and graduated with FIVE associates degrees! (Computer Science, Physics, Mathematics, and a few other Liberal Arts & Sciences Degrees). I am now working full time as a Front End Developer / E-Commerce Specialist at a company called AQLighting and I am actively pursuing my Bachelors Degree in Computer Science full time as well!
